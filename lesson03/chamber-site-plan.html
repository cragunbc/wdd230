Chamber of Commerce Site Plan

Site Specifications

Site Name: Syracuse Chamber of Commerce

Site Purpose: The Syracuse Chamber of Commerce acts as a central point to provide users with info of activites and buisness that belong to the Syracuse community as well as historical info on how the community came to be.

Scenarios:

  • What's been the population growth over the past 10 years?
  • When does the board of directors meet to present info on new businesses and activites?
  • How can I get involved in the community?
  • Who can I reach out to?

Color Schema:

  • White: Text color for header and footer
  • Gray: Background for header and footer
  • Black: Main body text color

Typography: Merriweather - Used for the entire site

Wireframes: Large View then Mobile View
